About Shrek Juice Center

Our Story

Shrek Juice Centre was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to make healthy living delicious and accessible to everyone. What started as a small juice bar in a neighborhood market has grown into one of the most beloved juice centers in the region.

Our founder, Mr. Shrek Patel, was passionate about the healing power of natural foods. After experiencing significant health improvements through a juice-based diet, he decided to share this gift with his community.

Today, we operate 15 outlets across the city and have served over 1 million customers. We've expanded our menu to include a wide variety of juices, smoothies, and healthy snacks, but our commitment to quality and freshness remains unchanged.
